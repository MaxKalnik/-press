RewardExpert Relaunches Website with New Features that Help Consumers Take Full Advantage of Their Rewards

Comes out of beta and introduces a new fleet of financial tools, data and services that simplify earning and spending rewards.

RewardExpert

NEW YORK, August 9, 2017 – RewardExpert – a web service dedicated to helping people navigate reward programs, airline miles and credit card points – today announced the company is coming out of beta mode with a website relaunch featuring a new line of tools and services that simplify earning and spending credit card and travel rewards. RewardExpert’s new website expands it services to encompass every aspect of a consumer’s rewards management and redemption needs, while remaining cost-free.

“Taking full advantage of rewards programs can be challenging. They often lack transparency and are riddled with fine print,” says RewardExpert CEO and co-founder Roman Shteyn. “RewardExpert is expanding its scope to demystify rewards programs and empower our users with tools and information to become savvy spenders who can make their money work for them.”

RewardExpert now offers three primary features:

  • RewardExpert® Tools – A fleet of smart tools that helps users throughout every step of their journey, from earning miles and points to redeeming for award travel.

    • RewardExpert® Travel

      This first-of-its-kind metasearch engine tool enables users to easily filter and find award flights based on their needs, while reducing an average of 40 minutes from the reward travel-planning process. RewardExpert® Travel is additionally integrated with a flight-pricing API from Skyscanner, allowing for easy comparisons between cash prices versus reward costs of flights.

    • RewardExpert® Shopping

      Many consumers are unaware they can earn up to 15X points by shopping through their reward program’s online portal. This tool helps users easily see where they can earn the most rewards while shopping at over 20,000 of their favorite stores.

    • RewardExpert® Trip

      Current users are familiar with this tool as it previously operated as the sole function of RewardExpert. Under its new name, RewardExpert® Trip, users are still able to easily manage all of their rewards programs via their RewardWallet and create customized strategies for redeeming award travel.

  • Data Intelligence Center – A series of actionable, data-driven reports designed to help consumers make better personal finance and travel decisions. From analyzing destinations based on specific interests to breaking down the nitty gritty numbers of rewards credit card; these insightful reports keep users in the know.

  • Financial Product Reviews – With a myriad of credit card and banking products on the market, RewardExpert combs through the fine print to provide comprehensive and informed reviews that help consumers pick products best suited to their needs.

RewardExpert’s new website will be regularly updated with new data reports, product reviews, and consumer advice. To experience RewardExpert’s new tools and features, visit www.rewardexpert.com.
